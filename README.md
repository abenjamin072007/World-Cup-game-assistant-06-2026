# World-Cup-game-assistant-06-2026
Some people might watch soccer because of others who ask them to watch it or because the game is on TV and they have nothing else to watch. Or, they just want to have fun. However, they may not know how the game works. This is the problem that I am trying to solve.
My technical approach was to build a "teach me the game" assistant for those who are interested in watching the game but do not know how it works. When a user types in a question, the answer is generated based on natural language processing. Essentially, the program looks at keywords from the question that the user types in and produces an answer based on what keywords were used. This is the AI part of the solution.
Having knowledge of soccer makes watching it more interesting and enjoyable. If people are able to gain knowledge of soccer, it enhances their experience and helps them understand the hype behind the World Cup. Also, if people have knowledge of how the World Cup works in general, they can see what their team has to accomplish in order to climb the ranks. Overall, my solution matters in the context of soccer and the FIFA World Cup because it enhances fans' experience.
